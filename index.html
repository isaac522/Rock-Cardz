Rock Stars

Gerry Garcia

Jerome John Garcia (August 1, 1942 - August 9, 1995) was an American musician known for being the principal songwriter, lead guitarist, and a vocalist with the rock band Grateful Dead.

Tina Turner

Tina Turner (1939 - 2023), the "Queen of Rock 'n' Roll", rose to fame with Ike Turner and had a huge solo career.

Frank Zappa

Frank Vincent Zappa (1940 - 1993) was a composer known for nonconformity, improvisation, and satirical music.

Dolores O'Riordan

Dolores Mary Eileen O'Riordan (1971 - 2018) was the lead vocalist of the alternative rock band The Cranberries.

Gwen Stefani

Gwen Renée Stefani (born 1969) is the lead singer of No Doubt, known for hits like "Just a Girl" and "Don't Speak".

Elvis Presley

Elvis Presley (1935 - 1977), the "King of Rock and Roll", remains one of the most influential cultural icons ever.
